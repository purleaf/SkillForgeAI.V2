Prepare for the IELTS Writing Test

Welcome to the IELTS Writing Test preparation. When you're ready, click Next to begin.

WRITING

This section measures your ability to write in English to communicate in an academic environment.

There are two writing tasks in this section.

  • Task 1: You will be presented with a situation and asked to write a response of at least 150 words.
  • Task 2: You will be asked to write an essay in response to a point of view, argument, or problem, with a minimum of 250 words.
20:00

Task 1

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.

You recently attended a conference and would like to give a presentation to your colleagues about it. Write a letter to your manager requesting permission to give a presentation. In your letter:

  • Describe what type of presentation you have in mind
  • What topics it would cover
  • Why it would be beneficial to your colleagues

Write at least 150 words.

Task 2

You should spend about 40 minutes on this task.

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programmes (for example, working for a charity, improving the neighbourhood or teaching sports to younger children).

To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

Write at least 250 words.
